Our Room: CS411

Every class, every breakthrough, every "why is my code not working" moment — it all happens in CS411. Our shared space, our shared identity.

Block 11PRG02 is part of Adamson University's Senior High School ICT strand under the Programming & Analytics specialization. We are Grade 11 students who chose this pathway out of genuine passion for technology, problem-solving, and building things that matter.

We code. We debug. We stay up late fixing errors. We collaborate, argue over semi-colons, and present work we're proud of. At the end of the day, we are one block with one mission — to become the developers and data analysts who will shape tomorrow's digital landscape. Formidable. Faithful. Adamson.
